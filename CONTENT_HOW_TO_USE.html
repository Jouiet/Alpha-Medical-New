How to Use Your Products - Guides & Videos

Get the most out of your Alpha Medical Care products with our comprehensive usage guides and video tutorials. Proper use ensures maximum effectiveness, comfort, and safety.

General Usage Guidelines

Before First Use:

  • Read all included instructions carefully
  • Wash hands before handling the product
  • Check for any damage or defects
  • Ensure you have the correct size
  • Consult your healthcare provider if you have any medical concerns

Safety First:

  • Start with shorter wear times (30-60 minutes)
  • Gradually increase duration as your body adjusts
  • Never wear so tight that it causes pain, numbness, or tingling
  • Remove immediately if you experience discomfort
  • Do not use on open wounds, infections, or severe swelling

Product-Specific Guides

Knee Braces & Supports

How to Wear:

  1. Sit down and position the brace centered over your kneecap
  2. The padded opening should align with your kneecap
  3. Wrap the upper strap first, then the lower strap
  4. Adjust for a snug but comfortable fit
  5. Stand and bend your knee to test - it should feel supportive but not restrictive

When to Use: During physical activity, walking, standing for long periods, or as recommended by your healthcare provider.

Care Instructions: Hand wash with mild soap, air dry completely before next use.

Back Support & Posture Correctors

How to Wear:

  1. Put on over a thin shirt for comfort
  2. Position the lower edge at your hips
  3. Pull shoulder straps over shoulders
  4. Adjust side straps for desired compression
  5. Stand straight and adjust until you feel gentle support

When to Use: While sitting at a desk, during daily activities, or when you notice yourself slouching. Start with 30-60 minutes daily.

Important: Don't rely on posture correctors exclusively - combine with core strengthening exercises.

Ankle & Wrist Supports

How to Wear:

  1. Slide foot or hand through the opening
  2. Position support centered over the joint
  3. Wrap straps in figure-8 pattern for ankle, straight for wrist
  4. Secure with velcro - snug but not tight
  5. Check that you can wiggle toes/fingers

When to Use: During sports, physical activity, or recovery from injury. Can be worn during daily activities for chronic conditions.

Care Instructions: Hand wash after each use, especially after sweating. Air dry thoroughly.

Compression Sleeves

How to Wear:

  1. Roll sleeve down like a sock
  2. Slide over limb starting from narrow end
  3. Gradually unroll upward, smoothing out wrinkles
  4. Ensure even compression throughout
  5. No bunching or rolling at edges

When to Use: During exercise, recovery periods, or as recommended for circulation support.

Duration: Can typically be worn for several hours, but remove if you notice any discomfort.

Therapy Equipment (Cupping Sets, Massage Devices)

Cupping Therapy Sets:

  1. Clean skin area thoroughly
  2. Apply light massage oil if desired
  3. Place cup on skin and activate suction mechanism
  4. Leave in place for 5-15 minutes
  5. Release suction gently before removing

Safety: Some bruising is normal. Start with lower suction levels. Do not use on injured or sensitive skin.

Massage Devices:

  • Read device-specific instructions carefully
  • Start on lowest setting
  • Use for recommended duration (typically 10-20 minutes)
  • Move device slowly over treatment area
  • Charge fully before first use

Common Fitting Issues & Solutions

Too Tight:
Symptoms: Numbness, tingling, skin discoloration, increased pain
Solution: Loosen straps immediately. If symptoms persist, remove and contact healthcare provider.

Too Loose:
Symptoms: Support slides or moves during activity, doesn't provide enough stability
Solution: Tighten straps. If still loose, you may need a smaller size.

Skin Irritation:
Symptoms: Redness, itching, rash
Solution: Wear over a thin layer of clothing. Ensure product is clean and dry. If irritation continues, discontinue use.

Rolls or Bunches:
Solution: Smooth out before securing. Ensure correct size. Some products work better over clothing.

Care & Maintenance

Cleaning:

  • Hand wash with mild soap and lukewarm water
  • Rinse thoroughly to remove all soap
  • Gently squeeze out excess water (don't wring)
  • Air dry flat or hanging - never use heat
  • Clean after each use, especially if sweating

Storage:

  • Store in cool, dry place
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Don't fold or compress tightly
  • Keep velcro straps fastened when not in use

When to Replace:

  • Velcro no longer holds securely
  • Material shows wear, tears, or stretching
  • Support no longer provides adequate compression
  • After 6-12 months of daily use

Exercises to Complement Your Support Products

Important: Support products help, but strengthening exercises are essential for long-term improvement.

For Knee Support Users:

  • Quad sets
  • Straight leg raises
  • Wall sits
  • Step-ups

For Posture Support Users:

  • Shoulder blade squeezes
  • Chest stretches
  • Core strengthening (planks)
  • Upper back rows

For Ankle Support Users:

  • Ankle circles
  • Calf raises
  • Balance exercises
  • Resistance band exercises

Consult a physical therapist for personalized exercise programs.

Medical Guidance

  • If pain worsens or doesn't improve
  • For personalized wear time recommendations
