Privacy & Data Protection

Your registration information is used solely for warranty tracking, customer support, and sending you product-related communications. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is registration mandatory?
No, registration is optional. However, we highly recommend it to take advantage of the extended warranty and exclusive benefits.

Can I register after 30 days?
Yes! You can register your product at any time during the original warranty period.

What if I lost my order number?
Contact us at support@alphamedical.shop with your purchase details (email, approximate date, product name) and we'll help you locate your order.

Do I need to register for warranty coverage?
No, your standard warranty is active from the date of purchase. Registration simply extends it and adds extra benefits.
