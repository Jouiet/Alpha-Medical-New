Defect-Free Guarantee
All products are inspected before shipping to ensure they meet our quality standards. If you receive a defective item, we'll replace it immediately at no cost to you.

Product Warranty Coverage

Standard Warranty (90 Days)
All products include a 90-day warranty covering manufacturing defects and material failures. This includes:

  • Stitching defects in braces and supports
  • Velcro or fastener failures
  • Material tears or breaks under normal use
  • Malfunctioning adjustable components

Extended Warranty (1 Year)
Premium products (therapy equipment, electronic devices) include a 1-year warranty covering:

  • Electronic component failures
  • Battery defects
  • Mechanical failures
  • Power adapter issues

What's Covered

Our warranty covers:

  • Manufacturing Defects: Issues that arise from faulty materials or workmanship
  • Normal Wear: Reasonable wear and tear during the warranty period
  • Functional Failures: Products that don't perform as intended
  • Shipping Damage: Items damaged during delivery

What's NOT Covered

The warranty does not cover:

  • Damage from misuse, abuse, or improper care
  • Normal wear and tear after warranty period
  • Damage from alterations or modifications
  • Loss or theft
  • Products purchased from unauthorized sellers
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect function
