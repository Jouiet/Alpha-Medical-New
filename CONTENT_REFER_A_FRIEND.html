Refer a Friend Program

Love your Alpha Medical Care products? Share the wellness with friends and family - and get rewarded for it!

How It Works

Step 1: Refer Your Friends
Share Alpha Medical Care with friends, family, or anyone who could benefit from our medical support products.

Step 2: They Get a Discount
Your friend receives 10% off their first order using your unique referral link or code.

Step 3: You Earn Rewards
When your friend makes a purchase, you receive a $10 store credit for your next order!

Referral Program Rules

Eligibility:

  • You must have made at least one purchase from Alpha Medical Care
  • Your account must be in good standing
  • Referred friends must be new customers (first purchase)
  • Referee must use your unique link or code at checkout

Reward Conditions:

  • Referral credit is issued after referred friend's order is delivered
  • Minimum purchase of $30 required to qualify
  • Credits appear in your account within 7 days of delivery
  • Credits are non-transferable and have no cash value
  • Returns or cancellations forfeit the referral credit

Limitations:

  • You cannot refer yourself or use your own code
  • Referrals from the same household/address may not qualify
  • Suspected fraud or abuse will result in disqualification
  • We reserve the right to modify or terminate the program at any time

Tracking Your Referrals

To check the status of your referrals and credits:

  1. Email support@alphamedical.shop
  2. Include "Check Referral Status" in subject
  3. Provide your email address and order number

We'll send you a summary of:

  • Total successful referrals
  • Current available credits
  • Pending referrals (orders not yet delivered)
  • Total rewards earned to date

Using Your Referral Credits

At Checkout:

  1. Add items to your cart as usual
  2. Proceed to checkout
  3. Enter your email address (must match your account)
  4. Your available credits will automatically appear
  5. Choose to apply credits to your order
  6. Complete your purchase

Credit Stacking:
You can use multiple referral credits on a single order! If you have $50 in credits and your order is $100, you'll only pay $50.

Credit Expiration:
Your credits never expire! Save them up for a big purchase or use them immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many friends can I refer?
Unlimited! There's no cap on how many people you can refer or how much credit you can earn.

When will I receive my credit?
Credits are issued 7 days after your friend's order is delivered and the return period has passed.

Can I combine referral credits with other discounts?
Yes! Referral credits can be combined with most promotions and sales.

What if my friend returns their order?
If your friend returns their order, the referral credit will be voided or deducted from your account.

Can I refer someone who shopped here before?
No, the program is for new customers only. Your friend must not have placed an order before.

What if my friend doesn't use my link?
They can enter your unique referral code at checkout instead. Make sure to share both your link and code!
