Important: Please read this medical disclaimer carefully before using any Alpha Medical Care products.

General Medical Disclaimer

The products sold by Alpha Medical Care are designed to provide support, compression, and relief for various musculoskeletal conditions. However, they are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition or disease.

Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional before using any medical support product, especially if you have:

  • A diagnosed medical condition
  • Chronic pain or injury
  • Recent surgery or trauma
  • Circulatory problems
  • Diabetes
  • Skin sensitivity or allergies
  • Pregnancy

Not a Substitute for Medical Care

Our products are supportive devices only and should not be used as a replacement for:

  • Professional medical diagnosis
  • Prescription medications
  • Physical therapy
  • Medical treatment or surgery
  • Professional rehabilitation programs

If you experience persistent or worsening pain, swelling, numbness, or other symptoms, stop using the product immediately and consult a healthcare professional.

Proper Use & Safety

Read Instructions Carefully
Always read and follow the included product instructions and safety guidelines. Improper use may result in discomfort, injury, or reduced effectiveness.

Start Gradually
When using a new support product, start with shorter wear times and gradually increase as your body adjusts.

Monitor Your Response
Pay attention to how your body responds. Some initial adjustment is normal, but severe discomfort, pain, skin irritation, or numbness are not.

Contraindications & Warnings

Do NOT use our products if you have:

  • Open wounds or infections in the area
  • Severe circulatory disorders
  • Deep vein thrombosis (DVT)
  • Severe peripheral arterial disease
  • Known allergies to product materials
  • Acute inflammation or swelling (unless directed by a doctor)

Special Populations:

  • Pregnancy: Consult your obstetrician before using support products
  • Children: Use only under adult supervision and medical guidance
  • Elderly: May require assistance with proper fitting and use
  • Diabetics: Monitor skin condition closely for pressure points or irritation

Potential Risks & Side Effects

While rare, potential side effects from improper use or overuse may include:

  • Skin irritation or rash
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Increased swelling
  • Circulatory problems
  • Muscle weakness from over-dependence
  • Allergic reactions to materials

If you experience any adverse effects, discontinue use immediately and seek medical attention.

Product Limitations

Our products have limitations and may not be suitable for:

  • Severe or unstable injuries
  • Post-surgical immobilization (use prescribed medical devices instead)
  • Conditions requiring custom orthotics
  • Sports performance enhancement beyond support and compression

Professional Medical Advice

Emergency Situations

Seek immediate medical attention if you experience:

  • Severe pain that worsens with product use
  • Sudden swelling or discoloration
  • Loss of sensation or movement
  • Signs of circulation problems (coldness, blueness)
  • Chest pain, shortness of breath, or dizziness
  • Signs of infection (fever, redness, warmth, pus)

In case of emergency, call 911 or your local emergency number immediately.
