Product Information

How do I choose the right size?
Each product page includes detailed sizing information. Measure the area indicated in the size chart and select accordingly. When in doubt, order one size up.

What materials are your products made from?
Most products use breathable neoprene, nylon, spandex, and latex-free materials. Specific materials are listed on each product page.

Are your products latex-free?
Yes, all Alpha Medical Care products are latex-free to minimize allergic reactions.

Can I wash my support product?
Most products are hand-washable with mild soap and water. Air dry only - do not machine wash or dry. Check individual product care instructions.

How long will my product last?
With proper care, most products last 6-12 months with daily use. Lifespan depends on frequency of use and care.

How to Use Your Products

Where can I find usage instructions?
Each product includes usage instructions in the package. You can also visit our How to Use / Videos page for detailed guides and video tutorials.

How tight should my brace/support be?
It should feel snug and supportive, but not painful or restrict circulation. You should be able to slide one finger under the edge. If you experience numbness, tingling, or increased pain, loosen it immediately.

How long should I wear it?
Start with 30-60 minutes and gradually increase wear time. Most products can be worn 2-8 hours daily. Consult your healthcare provider for specific recommendations.

Can I wear it while sleeping?
Generally not recommended unless specifically advised by your healthcare provider. Most supports are designed for daytime activity.

Should I consult a doctor?
Yes. Always consult with a healthcare professional before using medical support products, especially for diagnosed conditions or injuries. See our Medical Disclaimer.

Yes! We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase, return it for a full refund or exchange.

Should I register my product?
Yes! Product registration extends your warranty by 30 days and unlocks exclusive benefits.
